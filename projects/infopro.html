Mobile App Design Case Study

A detailed look into the design of a healthcare-focused mobile app to enhance data accessibility and user engagement.

Project Overview

This mobile app was developed to provide healthcare professionals with a powerful, user-friendly platform to access the latest research, clinical trials, and personalized insights. The design focused on delivering a clean, intuitive user experience optimized for mobile devices.

Competitor Analysis

Analysis revealed that existing platforms often lack user-friendly customization features, leading us to prioritize advanced filtering and personalized curation.

DynaMed and UpToDate are trusted tools for clinicians but lack the customization, trend-focused content, and advanced filtering that researchers need for a comprehensive, mobile-first experience.

Design Solutions

Our approach for the new mobile app, InfoPro, we prioritized user accessibility, personalized content delivery, and intuitive navigation, enabling healthcare professionals to access critical insights quickly and effectively.

Content Categorization & Filtering

Introduced advanced filtering options, enabling users to filter content by categories like clinical trials, publications, and patents. This enhanced content discoverability and allowed users to tailor their feed to specific areas of interest.

Customizable Notification Settings

Implemented a notification center where users can set preferences for topics and content types. Users can prioritize notifications by urgency, ensuring they receive only the most relevant updates.

Personalized Dashboard

Designed a user-centered dashboard with quick access to saved searches, favorite topics, and the latest updates tailored to user interests, increasing engagement and ease of use.

Detailed Search Filters

Enhanced the search functionality with detailed filters, allowing users to narrow down results by content type, organization, country, and other critical criteria, making research faster and more targeted.

Account Settings & Notifications

Developed intuitive account settings with notification control, giving users full autonomy over what information they receive and when, enhancing the overall user experience.

Advanced Date Filtering

Incorporated a powerful date filtering tool, enabling users to view updates within specific time frames. This feature is essential for professionals needing timely, relevant data.
