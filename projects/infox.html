InfoX Case Study

Exploring the design and development of InfoX, a real-time insights platform tailored for healthcare professionals and industry experts.

Project Overview

InfoX was designed to provide healthcare professionals with curated, real-time insights into industry trends, competitor movements, and regulatory changes. Our goal was to build an intuitive, data-rich platform that enhances decision-making and keeps users informed of relevant developments.

Research & Problem Identification

Through user interviews and competitor analysis, we identified a need for easily accessible, up-to-date information tailored to healthcare trends. Users expressed frustration with fragmented information and lack of customization in existing solutions.

Users highlighted a need for curated industry insights that are both timely and relevant, which informed our decision to focus on real-time updates and personalization.

Analysis revealed that existing platforms often lack user-friendly customization features, leading us to prioritize advanced filtering and personalized curation.

Design Solutions

Curated Industry Boards

Created dedicated boards for sectors like Competitor News, Medical Devices, and Regulatory News, allowing users to focus on specific areas of interest.

Real-Time Updates

Integrated real-time data sources to ensure that users receive the latest updates as they happen, enhancing the platform’s relevance and utility.

Advanced Search & Filters

Implemented robust filtering options to allow users to search by sources, date range, and topics, providing a highly customizable search experience.

Personalized Curation Hub

Developed a Curation Hub that recommends content based on the user's interests, making InfoX a more engaging and user-centered platform.

Prototyping & Feedback

Created high-fidelity prototypes to illustrate key workflows and interactions, enabling stakeholders to visualize the platform’s potential. User feedback on the prototype guided iterative improvements, particularly around navigation and content display.
